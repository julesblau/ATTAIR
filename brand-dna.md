# ATTAIRE Brand DNA

## Name Rationale
Keep **ATTAIRE**. Renamed from ATTAIR to ATTAIRE -- the extra E gives it a French fashion-house quality ("aire" as in ambiance/aura) while still sounding like "attire." Hides "AI" in plain sight without making it the whole personality. Pronounceable, memorable, hashtag-ready.

The wordmark renders the hidden **AI** with a single lime asterisk -- AI in plain sight, but treated as a graphic mark rather than a flex.

---

## Color System

### Background: Cream `#FAF7EE`
Warm off-white, the new default canvas. Light is the home base; dark is opt-in. Cream feels softer than pure white, reads as "feed" rather than "studio," and lets product imagery pop without competing.

### Accent: Lime `#C8FF3D`
The signature. Used for highlight chips inside headlines, the floating action button, badges, and active states. Always behaves like a marker on top of cream -- never as text, never as a background for body copy. High-energy, screen-native, instantly recognizable.

### Secondary Punch: Warm Orange `#FF6B3D`
Used sparingly. Reserved for "SCAN COMPLETE" markers, % confidence ticks, and small status callouts where lime would compete with a primary action. Never decorative.

### Text
- **Primary** `#1A1A1A` -- near-black, sits cleanly on cream
- **Secondary** `#6B6B6B` -- metadata, captions, supporting copy

### Surface
- **Card** `#FFFFFF` -- pure white cards float above the cream canvas. The cream/white step replaces the old dark/parchment contrast as the primary depth cue.

### Colors to Avoid
- Gold in any form (deprecated with the old identity)
- Pure black `#000` backgrounds (use `#1A1A1A` if dark mode is needed)
- Lime as text or large fill behind text -- it is a highlighter, not a surface
- Reds that read as error states

---

## Typography

### Display: Space Grotesk
700 weight, tight tracking (`-1px` to `-2px`), lowercase headlines. Used for hero lines like "see it. scan it. shop it." Geometric, slightly mechanical, screen-first. The lowercase + tight tracking combination is the typographic signature.

### Body: Inter
Weights 400-700. Neutral, dense-friendly, performs at the small sizes the app demands. Pairs cleanly with Space Grotesk without fighting it.

### Hierarchy
- **Hero headlines:** Space Grotesk 700, lowercase, tracking -1 to -2
- **Section titles:** Space Grotesk 700, 20-28px
- **Body:** Inter 400-500, 14-15px
- **Captions/Meta:** Inter 500, 11-12px
- **Wordmark:** custom SVG, lime asterisk in place of "AI"

---

## Logo System

### Wordmark (Primary)
ATTAIRE set in a heavy geometric sans, with the **AI** rendered as a single lime asterisk (`#C8FF3D`). The asterisk replaces the previous gold dot -- same "AI hidden in plain sight" idea, new energy. Black on cream by default; white on dark when inverted.

### Monogram/Icon
A bold geometric **A** for favicon and tab bar. Black on cream, or set inside a `#1A1A1A` circle for contexts that need a contained mark. No gold, no decoration.

---

## Visual Signatures

These are the patterns that make a screen unmistakably ATTAIRE:

1. **Lime chip headlines** -- one phrase inside a hero line gets a lime fill, rotated `-1.5deg`, like a marker swipe over the word. Used once per view, max.
2. **Pill nav and chips** -- 999px radius for navigation, filters, and tags. Cards use 18px (lg) or 12px (md). The radius scale is tight on purpose.
3. **Asymmetric, image-forward layout** -- product imagery dominates. Copy supports the image, not the other way around. Dense, app-like, closer to a Depop home feed than an editorial spread.
4. **Springy iOS motion** -- entrances use `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)` (overshoot spring); transitions use `cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)` (smooth ease). Movement is felt, not subtle.
5. **Orange status accents** -- "SCAN COMPLETE", confidence percentages, and tick markers in `#FF6B3D`. The only place orange shows up.

## Brand Voice

**One sentence:** ATTAIRE talks like the friend whose For You page you trust -- fast, direct, in on the joke.

**Two-sentence version:** ATTAIRE knows what brand that is and where to get it. We're hot, not haughty -- helpful, never preachy; clever, never cute.

### Tone Shift
The old voice leaned quiet-luxury. The new voice is youthful, social-native, Depop / TikTok Shop energy. Confident still, but warmer and more chronically online. Less "fashion house," more "best friend with great taste who's always shopping."

### Tone Attributes
- **Confident** not arrogant
- **Sharp** not cold
- **Playful** not random
- **Inclusive** not generic

### Words We Use
scan, find, match, fit, drop, look, snag, cop, vibe

### Words We Avoid
algorithm, synergy, leverage, utilize, disrupting, democratizing, curated (overused), elevated

---

## Target Feeling

> When someone opens ATTAIRE, they should feel like they just unlocked a cheat code for shopping the feed.

Not intimidated. Not asked to slow down. Just a tight, satisfying loop: **see something you like, scan it, find it, cop it.** The app should feel like the most fun tool on the home screen.

---

## Design Principles (for all future work)

1. **Speed is the product.** The fastest path to the answer is the most premium experience.
2. **Show, don't explain.** One outfit photo communicates more than five bullet points.
3. **Earn trust through accuracy.** Confidence percentages, web-verified results, transparent matching. No hand-waving.
4. **Addictive, not exploitative.** The loop (scan > find > save > scan again) should feel rewarding. No dark patterns, countdown timers, or artificial scarcity.
5. **Works for everyone.** A 19-year-old on TikTok and a 35-year-old in an office should both feel like this app was made for them.
