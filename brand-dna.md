# ATTAIRE Brand DNA

## Name Rationale
Keep **ATTAIRE**. Renamed from ATTAIR to ATTAIRE -- the extra E gives it a French fashion-house quality ("aire" as in ambiance/aura) while still sounding like "attire." Hides "AI" in plain sight without making it the whole personality. Pronounceable, memorable, hashtag-ready.

---

## Color System

### Primary: Warm Gold `#C9A96E`
Keep the gold. It reads as premium without being exclusionary, works on both dark and light backgrounds, and differentiates ATTAIRE from the sea of neon-accent consumer apps. Gold says "curated" not "cheap."

**Gradient range:** `#D4AF6A` (highlight) to `#B8965C` (shadow) for depth on icons and accents.

### Dark Background: `#0C0C0E`
Near-black with a warm undertone. Not pure `#000` -- avoids the harsh contrast that feels sterile. This is the default canvas.

### Light Mode Background: `#F6F4F0`
Warm parchment. Not hospital-white. Feels like a fashion magazine page.

### Secondary Accents
- **Soft Ivory** `#FAFAFA` -- primary text on dark, cards on light
- **Muted Stone** `#8A8580` -- secondary text, metadata
- **Confidence Tiers:**
  - Budget/Save: `#5AC8FF` (cool blue)
  - Mid/Best Value: `#C9A96E` (gold, the default)
  - Premium/Splurge: `#C77DFF` (soft purple)

### Colors to Avoid
- Neon green, hot pink, or saturated primaries that feel like gaming/gambling
- Pure white `#FFF` as a background (too clinical)
- Any red that could be confused with error states

## Typography

### Primary: Outfit (Google Fonts)
Already in use. Clean geometric sans-serif with personality. Excellent weight range from 300-800. Reads well at small mobile sizes and feels contemporary without being trendy-disposable.

### Display/Logo: Instrument Serif (italic)
Currently used for the wordmark. Keep as an optional editorial accent for hero moments -- section titles, pull quotes in scan results. Its italic form adds movement and fashion-editorial energy.

### Hierarchy
- **Headlines:** Outfit 700, 20-28px
- **Body:** Outfit 400, 14-15px
- **Captions/Meta:** Outfit 500, 11-12px, letter-spacing 0.5px
- **Logo:** Instrument Serif Italic, 22px (or custom SVG wordmark)

## Logo System

### Wordmark (Primary)
Three options provided in GOAT/ZARA minimal wordmark style:

1. **Option 1 -- GOAT-style Bold:** Heavy-weight geometric sans-serif (Helvetica Neue Black/900). Ultra-tight letter spacing, no decoration, no gold. Pure white on transparent. Maximum confidence.

2. **Option 2 -- ZARA-style Thin Serif:** Thin elegant serif (Times New Roman, weight 300). Extremely tight letter spacing with near-overlapping characters. Fashion-house masthead energy.

3. **Option 3 -- Hybrid:** Medium-weight geometric sans-serif (Helvetica Neue Bold/700). Clean and minimal like GOAT, but with one subtle accent: a gold (#C9A96E) dot on the I. Restrained, distinctive.

### Monogram/Icon (Favicon, Tab Bar)
A bold geometric "A" in heavy sans-serif, set inside a dark circle (#0C0C0E) for the favicon. No gold, no crossbar -- pure GOAT-style minimalism. The standalone monogram (icon-attaire.svg) uses the same A without the circle background.

## Brand Voice

**One sentence:** ATTAIRE talks like a stylish friend who knows where to find everything -- confident, concise, never condescending.

**Two-sentence version:** ATTAIRE is the friend who always knows what brand that is and where to get it for less. We are helpful, never preachy; knowledgeable, never pretentious.

### Tone Attributes
- **Confident** not arrogant
- **Helpful** not hand-holdy
- **Sharp** not cold
- **Inclusive** not generic

### Words We Use
scan, find, match, style, look, discover, curated

### Words We Avoid
algorithm, synergy, leverage, utilize, disrupting, democratizing

---

## Target Feeling

> When someone opens ATTAIRE, they should feel like they just found a shortcut that the most stylish people already know about.

Not intimidated. Not overwhelmed by options. Just a clean, fast, almost addictive loop: **see something you like, scan it, find it, buy it.** The app should feel like a power tool disguised as a beautiful object.

---

## Design Principles (for all future work)

1. **Speed is luxury.** The fastest path to the answer is the most premium experience.
2. **Show, don't explain.** One outfit photo communicates more than five bullet points.
3. **Earn trust through accuracy.** Gold "Confirmed" badges, web-verified results, transparent confidence levels. No hand-waving.
4. **Addictive, not exploitative.** The loop (scan > discover > save > scan again) should feel rewarding. No dark patterns, countdown timers, or artificial scarcity.
5. **Works for everyone.** A 19-year-old on TikTok and a 35-year-old in an office should both feel like this app was made for them.
